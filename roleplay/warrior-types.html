Warrior types

Players choose a way of combat. This will affect their role in battle, weapons and armour allowed, armour and finaly the abilities.

Shieldbrother/Maiden

You are a normal warrior who learned to wield a axe, sword or a spear with a shield to protect yourself?
A warrior is known to be in a direct contact while fighting and to protect others with his shield and work as a main core of a battle.
Weapons/Armour:

  • Swords
  • Spear
  • Clubs
  • Shields
  • All Armours
  • Axe (If part of the Bluecloak)

Ability:

  • Valkyrie (Flint)
  • Duelist (Thistle)
  • Shaman (Greydwarf Eye)

Skirmisher

You support your warriors by flanking enemies with ranged weapons or by getting in their rear and backstab them.
A Skirmisher knows how to aim and hit his targets with a deadly eye.
Weapons/Armour:

  • Bows
  • Knives
  • Ooze Bombs
  • Spear
  • Leather Armour, Trollhide Armour (No cape allowed)
  • Metal helmets

Ability:

  • Ranger (Raw Meat)
  • Enchanter (Resin)
  • Rogue (Honey)

Berserker

An insane fighter bound to the nature and to its power lend for the fight.
You always thought the where in trance while fighting but here you will figure out they always where able to fight with the power of Nature.
Weapons/Armour:

  • Atgeir
  • Stagbreaker
  • Staghammer
  • Battleaxe
  • Longsword
  • All Armours

Ability:

  • Berserker (Bone Fragments)
  • Valkyrie (Flint)
  • Druid (Dandelion)
