The Tasty Mead

When the smell of warm mead reached the Gods in Ásgard, Skúld the Norn appreared to the brave Farmers. For she had heard the Jarl had called for banquet three days from now. The preparations for the warriors' first great task were at hand and the event was surely woven into the tapestry of fate. But not all was done. Some things remained and so Skúld spoke to Helga:

“Mead is not only fermented honey with hops and herbs. The drink is a channel through which one speaks to the Gods. And who knows, sometimes they speak to the drinker.”

“Prepare a place in the meadows. Make it beautiful to the face of the Gods. Then, each must speak a rhyme in offering. Only then will the Mead have its powers for what is to come. Bring it all to the banquet then.”

"Remember, the knife, the mead, the surtling stones. All must be there."
