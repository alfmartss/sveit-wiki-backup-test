The Age of the Elder has begun

With the slaying of Eikthyr the warriors had finally secured a relative peace on their island and it was now time to explore this strange world of Valheim. After the Jarl had commissioned three Karve to be built, an expedition was mounted: Ragnulf the Hunter had told them that his remaining crew had ventured to an eastward island and established an outpost there.

It was a lead as good as any so the warriors left their shores in search of others. Eventually they found the outpost Ragnulf's men had built but it was in ruin and under constant siege by the filthy Greydwarfs. Still, the Jarl's men persisted and managed to rebuild a good portion of the outpost and then set foot on the new lands in search of an important errand from Heimdall, the watchful God.

The questions now were: What happened to Ragnulf's men and what happened to the errand to the Jarl? Would the island reveal its secrets? And what other things would they find? The Greydwarf threat at home was far from over but Ulgríf the Skogamaðr had not shown himself yet. Would he wait until all the good fighters were off fighting their wars in other lands? Fate, it seemed, didn't make a decision easy. It never did...
