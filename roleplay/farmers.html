Farmers FAQ

Cauldron: can I build & use a Cauldron?

You need a smith to build it for you or you can use a shared facility.

You can use it to craft anything from it.

 

Crafting: apart from food what other things can I craft?

Meads and potions.

 

Farming: what things can I grow/farm?

Raspberries, carrots, red mushrooms, turnips, wild boars, tamed boars, fish, honey, dandelion, barley.

 

Fermenter: can I build & use a Fermenter?

You need a smith to build it for you or you can use a shared facility.

Craft any mead or potion.

 

Services: what kind of services can I do for other people?

Using cultivator, cauldron, fermenter.
