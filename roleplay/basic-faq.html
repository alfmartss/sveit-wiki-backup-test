Basic roleplaying

Can I do something that isn't explicitly forbidden in the rules?
First ask yourself if it fits the profession/group and your character description
If you still aren't sure ask at RP-QUESTION rooms
Don’t be a dick.

Boss: Can we kill a boss when we want?
No, Only if you're on a quest directed by the Jarl.

Build: Can I build anywhere as i like?
No, you can build a one-storey 5x6 house and only ground floor high, in the town or outside. You must ask the Jarl your chosen spot.
Build simple furniture (table, stool, chair)
For more elaborate furniture you should ask the foresters or smiths
For Upgrades (more space) ask permission from Jarl.
Stone builds: permission from Stonecutter

Cooking station: can I roast some meat?
Yep.

Cauldron:
Farmers use cauldrons.

Dungeons: Can I raid dungeons as I like?
People will have to turn up to the Jarl and get permission to do dungeons.
Instead of going on your own, find an event raid or group.

Fermenter:
Farmers use fermenter.

Fish: Can I go fishing?
Yes.

Food: Where can I get food?
Everyone knows how to kill a beast and roast its meat. For a more peaceful option just go for a walk and get a berry from a bush.
Some professions have more skills about other types of food.(look in their specific FAQ).
You can buy it from them.

Metal: Can I craft metal things if I'm not a smith?
No.

Ore: Can I mine ore if I'm not a smith?
Only if you're on a mining mission directed by a smith.

Portals: Can I build a portal ?
Nobody can build portals and they are deactivated anyway.

Repair: How do I repair metal things?
You ask a smith.

Runestones: Where can I get runes?
Runestones will be sold by the jarl and lawspeaker. They can also be event rewards or traded with foreign merchants occasionally.

Skúld (Character)
Skúld is one of the three Norns of Norse mythology. On "Sveit RP" she is the Gamemaster figure. As such she gives out quests, triggers events or just keeps watching the warriors (i.e. you). If she is around and doesn't speak, consider her "not there". Also, don't be confused: Skúld can reach places that are "off" for mortals; for the time being at least.

Traveling: Can I travel wherever I want?
Only in starting island
Apart from that you should have to ask permission to the Jarl

Taxes: Do I have to pay taxes?
Taxes are paid as a group.. not per character.
You play as a group, you pay as a group, and will be depending on earnings, between 200-500 coins/week.
It's paid in coins, earned by selling things.

Ward: Can I build a ward?
The ward needs a ceremony by the town’s priest. So you need to ask the Jarl.

Weapons.What type of weapons can I craft?
Stone age weapons. Including wood arrows.
To use the axe as a weapon you should ask permission to the Jarl.

Wood: Can I get wood?
Wood is a resource limited to the foresters, you have to buy it from them.
We never, NEVER fell Oaks, as they are sacred!

Workbench: Can I build and use one?
Just a basic one, without any upgrade.
