General Rules: 

  • Wheaton’s Law (Don’t be a dick!) 
  • No spawn camping or building of spawn farms for grinding stuff 
  • No constant self deafen on Mumble 
  • Do not play in a way that does not make sense, breaking character, or otherwise doing something not suitable for the world. 
  • Do not use Information out of the Discord “OOC-Rooms” in-game 
  • Value of Life, punishment is hard and dying will now be painful too 
  • Check the FaQ (#faq) for more informations 
  • Use of abilitys outside of a fight is forbidden! 
  • No beds in front of dungeons etc. 

 

Roleplay Rules:

  • Names origin has be Scandinavian (Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Iceland)
  • Stick to your character sheet when playing
  • Focus on village life and cooperation between rural and town people, talking and just hanging out can be nice
  • Restricted tools and weapons by its profession 
    • Farmer: Cultivator, Cauldron, Windmill, Spinning Wheel 
    • Forester: Workbench upgraded, Cultivator, Axe, Kilin 
    • Blacksmiths: Forge, Smelter, Blast furnace
  • Stone constructions to a minimum, allowed with permission of the Jarl
  • No repairing of things you can not build or not allowed to build

 

Jarl's Rules:

  • Buildings, max. 5x6 tiles and one floor. Extensions via payment to the Jarl
  • No bigger terraforming
  • Max one house/building per person. Not included is needed workspace
  • As long as the bluecloaks patrol, no armour above leather allowed
  • Graves are not to be dug up. Do not create massive holes
  • Portals and Wards are forbidden
  • Explorations, Dungeons, Quests and Boss killing called by the Jarl only
  • Leaving the island is forbidden, except for explorations allowed by the Jarl
  • Loot found on Explorations, in Dungeons or on Quests are to be given to the Jarl. Jarl's split will be taken
  • Selling of things not belonging to your profession is forbidden, except for dropped loot like Tools, Weapons, Armours
  • No feeling oak trees
  • Cutting trees only allowed by Foresters
