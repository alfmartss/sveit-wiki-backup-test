Short guide how to get prepared to join our server. 

 

Preparing the mods

  1. Download and Install the R2ModMan
  2. Start R2ModMan and create a Profile.
  3. Click “Settings” and locate to “Locations”, setup the “Valheim directory” to your Valheim folder. (located in your SteamLibrary)
  4. Download our ModPack
  5. Locate in your R2ModMan to “Settings - All" and open “Browse profile folder”
  6. Unpack the Zipfolder into this location.
  7. Prepare your Mumble (positional audio)
  8. Close the folder and press “Start modded” to start up the game.
  9. Create your character with the given name.
  10. Join our server (IP gets automatically inserted) by using PW: Madur
