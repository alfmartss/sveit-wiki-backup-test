As of version 0.7.8 of EpicLoot there have been a number of changed item traits and also new ones. Some of them are very specialised and others have a more general bonus. In order to understand and judge the “value” of a trait you can find a full list of them here. They are taken from the translations.json file from the EpicLoot plugin folder.

Some of the specific traits depend on the player being at “critical health”, meaning it will only trigger if the player's current health drops below 30%. To compensate, such bonuses are bigger than the regular ones.
